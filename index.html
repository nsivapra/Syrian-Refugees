Syria is a country of political, humanitarian and armed conflict today. Bombings are destroying crowded cities and horrific human rights violations are widespread. Basic necessities like food and medical care are sparse. Due to international intervention and acceptance of Syrian citizens around 11 million Syrian emigrants have found asylum in different countries over the past years. Between the years 2010 to 2014 there has been a considerable spike in the magnitude of Syrian emigrants being accepted in some of the top countries.
